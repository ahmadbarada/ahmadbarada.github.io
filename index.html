My Story

At 21 years old, I'm a Lebanese personal trainer dedicated to helping others achieve their fitness goals. Currently, I'm proud to be a Personal Trainer at The Fit House, one of Lebanon's premier gym facilities.

My journey in fitness is complemented by my background in Hotel Management at LU, which has taught me the importance of service excellence and client satisfaction. I've also completed my Personal Training certification at CIS, where I honed my skills and knowledge.

I'm 6-month certified in both Resistance Training and Aerobic Training Techniques & Programs. This comprehensive training allows me to design effective, personalized workout programs that deliver real results.

Beyond my role at The Fit House, I offer freelance training services, bringing professional fitness guidance directly to clients who prefer personalized, one-on-one training experiences.
